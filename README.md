# Maths Creative Work

A small interactive website for my `MATHS102` creative project.

This piece reflects on my changing relationship with maths, from early confidence, to time away from it, to returning with more purpose. Instead of being just a written reflection, it presents that story through a clean visual layout and a simple calculus interaction.

## What It Includes

- a personal reflection on how maths has shaped me
- an interactive timeline of my journey with mathematics
- a live graph of `y = x^2` with a moving tangent line
- a simple example of calculus, change, and growth

## Files

- [`index.html`](/Users/jotinderbhamra/maths-creative-work/index.html) for the page content and structure
- [`styles.css`](/Users/jotinderbhamra/maths-creative-work/styles.css) for the design and layout
- [`script.js`](/Users/jotinderbhamra/maths-creative-work/script.js) for the timeline and graph interaction

## Why I Made It

The goal of this project is to show that maths is not just formulas and answers. For me, it is also connected to confidence, challenge, patience, and personal growth. The graph helps bring that idea to life by showing change visually, not just describing it in words.

## How To View It

Open [`index.html`](/Users/jotinderbhamra/maths-creative-work/index.html) in a browser and interact with the slider in the graph section.
